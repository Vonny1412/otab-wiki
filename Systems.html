OTAB Systems

OTAB introduces several gameplay systems that expand Valheim's creature mechanics such as taming, feeding, reproduction and creature interaction.

These systems define how creatures behave, how players interact with them, and under which conditions certain actions become possible.

Custom Procreation System

OTAB replaces Valheim's simplified reproduction logic with a more structured procreation system.

Creatures form temporary pair bonds, reproduction follows defined cycles, and additional rules such as mourning phases help prevent unrealistic partner switching.
