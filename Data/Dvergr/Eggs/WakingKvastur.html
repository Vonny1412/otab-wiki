Waking Kvastur

Galdr Craftable Grow time: About 5 min
Result: Kvastur

Overview

Waking Kvastur is a ritual item used to awaken a tamed Kvastur after approximately five minutes.

The ritual item can be crafted by hand using a Kvastur trophy, Ancient Bark, Resin, and Greydwarf Eyes.

The Kvastur trophy can be obtained by defeating Kvastur found in or around the Bog Witch hut in the Swamps.

Notes

All ritual items follow the same principle: the item will usually hatch back into itself and only rarely transform into the creature.

The player must remain nearby for the ritual to complete. Leaving the area and returning later will only advance the current growth step rather than completing the entire process.

The Wacky’s Database mod must be installed for the crafting recipe to be available.
