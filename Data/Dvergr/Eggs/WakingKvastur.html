Waking Kvastur

Ritual Trophy Craftable Grow time: Approx 5 min
Result: Kvastur

Overview

waking kvastur is a ritual item that will turn into a tamed Kvastur after approx 5 minutes. the ritual item can be crafted by hand using the kvastur trophy, antient bark, resin and greydwarf eyes. the kvastur trophy can be acuired by killing kvastur in or outside the witch hut in the swamps.

Notes

all ritual items follow the same principe where the item mosttly hatches into ittself and maybe by any chance into the creature. the player need to be present for the ritual item hatch into the creature. leaving the area and coming back after a while will just finish the current unguarantteed grow step but not the whole growing.
