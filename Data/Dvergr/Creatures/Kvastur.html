Kvastur

Not tameable Commandable

Overview

Kvastur is a wandering broom spirit that helps keep areas clean. Any item dropped on the ground nearby will eventually be swept away.

Kvastur can be obtained by completing a Waking Kvastur ritual.

Feeding

Every 10 seconds, Kvastur searches within a radius of 10 meters for items lying on the ground and consumes them.

Aggression

A tamed Kvastur does not take part in combat.

Warning

Players should be careful not to drop important items near Kvastur. He will sweep away anything lying on the ground without distinction.
