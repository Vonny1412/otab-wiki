Forest – Greydwarf Family

The Greydwarf Family represents creatures bound to the roots and stones of the forest. According to old tales, Greydwarfs are the twisted souls of murderers and oathbreakers, drawn into the depths of the woods and given new form by the ancient roots that run beneath the land.

Though crude and spiteful, they are not mindless. Greydwarfs recognize their own kind and gather in small forest clans. Shamans and Brutes hold a special role among them, guiding the others and tending to the dark growths from which new Greydwarfs may emerge.

Taming

All creatures of the Greydwarf family can be tamed, and only after The Elder has been defeated.

Reproduction

Only Brutes and Shamans are capable of reproduction. Instead of laying eggs, they produce buds.

Buds do not require shelter or incubation. Once the growth process is complete they hatch directly into fully grown creatures.

The resulting bud type depends on the partner combination and probability.

Most common outcome:

  • Greyling Bud

Less common:

  • Greydwarf Bud

Conditional outcomes:

  • Brute + Brute → may produce Brute Bud, never Shaman Bud
  • Shaman + Shaman → may produce Shaman Bud, never Brute Bud
  • Brute + Shaman → mostly Brute Bud, occasionally Shaman Bud
  • Shaman + Brute → mostly Shaman Bud, occasionally Brute Bud

Aggression

Tamed members of the Greydwarf family remain peaceful toward the player.

However, once a Grey is bound, it becomes hostile toward other creatures of the Forest faction, including wild Greys.

In the eyes of the forest, the creature no longer belongs to its kin.

Notes

All members of the Greydwarf family consume only Yellow Mushrooms. These mushrooms are primarily found in Burial Chambers beneath the Black Forest and in Sunken Crypts hidden within the Swamps.

Their bright yellow glow suggests that they may have been influenced by the presence of the dead within these ancient tombs. The mushrooms appear to absorb something from their surroundings, changing them in unusual ways.

In OTAB, this mysterious connection inspired the idea that Greydwarfs might be influenced by these mushrooms. As a result, Brutes and Shamans are able to produce buds, from which new Greydwarfs may emerge instead of being born in a traditional way.

The Greydwarf family is the first group the player encounters that turns against its own kind once tamed.
