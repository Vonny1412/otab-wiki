Deer

Tameable Not commandable Fed duration: 10 min
Offspring: Fawn

Overview

Deer are peaceful wildlife creatures commonly found in Meadows and Black Forest. They are naturally timid and will usually flee when approached by players or other threats.

In Of Taming and Breeding, deer can be tamed using plant-based food. Once tamed they remain passive, cannot be commanded, and will eventually produce Fawn offspring when paired with another deer.

Tamed deer do not stay near their original spawn point. They may wander very far over time. Players who want to keep them in a specific area should build a fence or enclosure around them.

Feeding

Deer accept a variety of plant-based food items. Crops such as carrots, turnips, and barley are their preferred food and provide a slightly longer fed duration.

Berries and mushrooms are also accepted, while simple plants such as dandelion and thistle are less nutritious and keep the deer fed for a shorter time.

Taming

Deer do not have any special taming mechanics. As long as they have access to food and are not disturbed, they will gradually become tamed over time.

Once tamed, deer remain passive and cannot be commanded. They behave similarly to wild deer, but will stay friendly toward players and other tamed creatures.

Breeding

When two tamed deer are nearby and well fed, they can reproduce and create a Fawn. The offspring spawns already tamed and can grow into a full adult deer.

Aggression

Deer are completely non-aggressive creatures. They will never consider other creatures as enemies, and creatures of the same faction will also never attack them.

This behavior remains unchanged even when creatures become tamed.
