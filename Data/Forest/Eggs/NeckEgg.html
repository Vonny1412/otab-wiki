Neck Egg

Straight growth Grow time: 30 min Requires: Water Seasonal: Spring, Summer, Fall
Result: Neck Hatchling (100%)

Overview

Neck Egg is a hatchable egg laid by Neck. It produces a Neck Hatchling.

The hatchling always emerges tamed, making this egg a straightforward way to raise a young neck.

Eggs float on the water surface, which makes them easy to spot and collect.

Hatching

Neck Egg takes 30 minutes to fully grow.

Environment

Neck Egg must be placed in water in order to grow. It does not need a nearby fire, does not need a roof, and has no minimum cover requirement.

This makes it easier to hatch than many other eggs, but players should still choose the location carefully. A safe shoreline, shallow pond, or enclosed water area is usually the best choice.

Requirements

Neck Egg can only grow during Spring, Summer, or Fall.

Notes

The seasonal restriction is only applied when the Seasons mod is installed. During winter, water surfaces freeze when that mod is active, which would prevent the egg from being placed in water. For this reason, Neck Eggs can only grow during Spring, Summer, and Fall.
