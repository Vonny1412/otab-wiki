Overview

Fawn is the juvenile form of Deer.

Fawns can also be petted and renamed immediately.

Growth

Fawn takes 50 minutes to grow up. Once growth is complete, it becomes an adult Deer. The tame state is preserved during this transition.

Aggression

Like Deer, Fawns are completely non-aggressive creatures. They never consider other creatures as enemies, and creatures of the same faction will also never attack them.
