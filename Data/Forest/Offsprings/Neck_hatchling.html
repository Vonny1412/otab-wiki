Neck Hatchling

Petable Grow-up time: 50 min Inherits tame state
Result: Neck

Overview

Neck Hatchling is the juvenile form of Neck. It is created by hatching a Neck Egg.

Neck Hatchlings can also be petted and renamed immediately.

Growth

Neck Hatchling takes 50 minutes to grow up. Once growth is complete, it becomes an adult Neck. The tame state is preserved during this transition.

Aggression

Wild creatures of the same faction may still treat Neck Hatchlings as hostile.
