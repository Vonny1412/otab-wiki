Greyling Bud

Straight growth Grow time: 30 min Defeated: The Elder
Result: Greyling (100%)

Overview

Greyling Bud is a hatchable bud produced by Greydwarf Brute or Greydwarf Shaman. It always grows into a Greyling.

Unlike animal eggs, Buds are part of the unusual reproduction of the Greydwarf Family.

Environment

Greyling Buds do not require any special environment to grow. They can hatch anywhere and do not need water, fire, shelter, or biome-specific conditions.

Requirements

Greyling Buds require The Elder to be defeated before they can grow.

Notes

The global key requirement helps prevent cross-server exploitation. Buds brought from another world should not bypass the intended progression.
