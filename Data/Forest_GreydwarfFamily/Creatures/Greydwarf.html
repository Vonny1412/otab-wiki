Greydwarf

Tameable Commandable Fed duration: 10 min Defeated: The Elder

Overview

Greydwarf is a wild creature of the forest.

It can be tamed but cannot reproduce.

Feeding

Greydwarfs consume Yellow Mushrooms.

Due to the animation used, it looks as if the Greydwarf simply throws the mushroom away instead of eating the whole thing.

Aggression

Tamed Greys are hostile toward creatures of the Forest faction, including all wild Greydwarfs.
