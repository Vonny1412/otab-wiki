Greydwarf Shaman

Tameable Commandable Fed duration: 10 min Defeated: The Elder
Offspring: Multiple bud types

Overview

Greydwarf Shaman is a powerful member of the Greydwarf Family. Like Greyling, Greydwarf and Greydwarf Brute, it can be tamed and commanded.

Feeding

Greydwarf Shamans consume Yellow Mushrooms.

When consuming Yellow Mushrooms, the Shaman briefly staggers and waves its limbs, almost as if the mushrooms have a slightly intoxicating effect.

Taming

Greydwarf Shamans can only be tamed after The Elder has been defeated.

Once tamed, they become commandable and will stay near their original spawn area or patrol point.

Breeding

Greydwarf Shamans can reproduce with both Greydwarf Shamans and Greydwarf Brutes. Instead of eggs, they produce buds.

The resulting bud type depends on the partner combination. Greyling Buds are the most common result, while stronger bud types are less common.

Aggression

Tamed Greys are hostile toward creatures of the Forest faction, including all wild Greydwarfs.
