Greydwarf Brute

Tameable Commandable Fed duration: 10 min Defeated: The Elder
Offspring: Multiple bud types

Overview

Greydwarf Brute is a powerful member of the Greydwarf Family. Unlike Greyling and Greydwarf, it can be tamed and commanded, just like Greydwarf Shamans.

Feeding

Greydwarf Brutes consume Yellow Mushrooms.

When consuming Yellow Mushrooms, the Brute briefly staggers and waves its limbs, almost as if the mushrooms have a slightly intoxicating effect.

Taming

Greydwarf Brutes can only be tamed after The Elder has been defeated.

Once tamed, they become commandable and will stay near their original spawn area or patrol point.

Breeding

Greydwarf Brutes can reproduce with both Greydwarf Brutes and Greydwarf Shamans. Instead of eggs, they produce buds.

The resulting bud type depends on the partner combination. Greyling Buds are the most common result, while stronger bud types are less common.

Aggression

Tamed Greys are hostile toward creatures of the Forest faction, including all wild Greydwarfs.
