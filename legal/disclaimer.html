Unofficial modification

Of Taming and Breeding (OTAB) is a community-created mod for Valheim. It is not an official expansion and is not affiliated with, approved by, or endorsed by the developers of the game.

The goal of OTAB is to extend Valheim’s existing gameplay systems while respecting the design philosophy of the original game. Wherever gameplay behavior is expanded or replaced, care is taken to keep the mechanics consistent with Valheim’s vanilla experience.

Use of game assets

OTAB does not introduce completely new assets such as models, animations, creatures, or original game content. Instead, the mod works exclusively with assets that already exist in Valheim.

Using the Jötunn modding framework, existing game prefabs are cloned and adjusted to create new gameplay interactions.

Modified icons

Some icons used in OTAB have been visually modified to clearly distinguish them from their original sources.

  • Some egg icons have been tinted.
  • Certain ritual items (such as "Waking ..." items) were manually edited to visually separate them from the original trophy icons.

These modified icons are not official assets created by the Valheim developers, and they are not entirely original creations either. They are derivative works based on existing Valheim assets and are used only for visual clarity within the mod.
